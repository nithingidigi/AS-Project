a word about us

AMARAVATHI SCHOOL was founded in 2021 by the school’s Chairman, Suresh Sir, an eminent leader in education, and is one of the most reputable, independent, selective, boarding and day school. Suresh Sir is also the Chairman and Founder of Amaravathi Group Og schools, which have become a model that many schools seek to replicate under his advice.

Our focus is on enhancing each child’s character and personality, and ensuring that an Amaravathian is a well-rounded, confident individual prepared for all the challenges and opportunities that the world will offer
